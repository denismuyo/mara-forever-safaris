Our Story

How We Came to Be

Mara Forever Safaris was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to share Africa's wild beauty through responsible, luxury safari experiences rooted in authenticity.

Our founders and guides grew up immersed in East Africa's landscapes, wildlife, and cultures. This lifelong connection shapes every itinerary we design — with precision, respect, and deep understanding of what makes a safari truly extraordinary.

We believe luxury is not just about exceptional lodges or fine dining — it is about meaningful moments:

  • Watching elephants move across golden savannah plains
  • Witnessing the Great Migration river crossings
  • Locking eyes with a mountain gorilla in its natural habitat

Our mission is to craft these moments while preserving the ecosystems and communities that make them possible.

Our Responsibility

Our Commitment to Conservation & Community

At Mara Forever Safaris, conservation is not a marketing statement — it is a responsibility. We actively support wildlife protection initiatives across East Africa, with a particular focus on rhino conservation and ecosystem preservation.

Rhino Tracking & Conservation Experiences

Our specialized rhino-tracking safaris provide guests with insight into anti-poaching efforts and endangered species protection within protected conservancies, directly supporting the rangers and programs keeping these animals safe.

Community & Education Initiatives

We support local education and environmental awareness programs to ensure tourism positively impacts the communities that call these regions home, creating long-term opportunity alongside the wildlife experiences we offer.

Responsible safari travel creates opportunity — not disruption.
